Reginalds LED Lighting has expanded its management team with the appointment of Sean Grennis as group sales director.

Grennis was previously head of sales at Bills Incandescent Lighting here in Brooks, where he managed a team of 75. In the new role he will work with the company to ensure that they achieve significant growth over the next financial year.

“The team at  Reginalds LED Lighting produce such amazing products all year round, so I cannot wait to start shouting out about their hard work,” said Grennis.

Tim Fritz, VP here at Reginalds added: “We are delighted to welcome Sean to the Reginalds LED Lighting Group senior management team and know that he will do a great job overseeing sales activity across the company. This is going to be a big year for us with some new LED technology coming out especially around Christmas, so we can’t wait to see how much the Company can grow over the next 12 months”.

Reginalds LED Lighting specializes in LED lighting for your home, for the office and someday, on Mars.
