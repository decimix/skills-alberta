The Reginalds LED Lighting brand might already be familiar — it is carried in a few stores across Canada and there are 200 stores internationally — but this November is the first time you'll be seeing an entire store in Edmonton Alberta dedicated to the LED Christmas Lights line.

Canada's first Reginalds LED Lighting  store started with Reginald Walter's family vacation to Disneyland in 2008. While there, the Brooks-ite stopped into a store where he saw a glowing light store and decided Brooks should have its own.

He partnered with his friend and coworker, Jody Refvik, and the two dog handlers quit their jobs to go into retail. Reginald and Jody contacted China's head office in Manzhouli in April 2009 and have been hosting private parties at friends' homes and fitness facilities with trunks full of LED product to build up interest ever since. 

Now, they have a store in West Edmonton Mall dedicated soley to Christmas LED lighting. The 850-square-foot space is on the second floor across from Crate & Barrel and Restoration Hardware.

The lights are designed to take you away to a distant Christmas land, the lights are so breathtaking.  Think several different fits for single storey, two storey, condo, duplex and townhouses. 

Christmas will never be the same.
